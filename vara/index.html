FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about membership and our services.

Who can become a member of Vara Siddhi?

+

Any Karnataka resident who is 18 years or older can apply for membership. You need a valid Aadhaar card, address proof, and a minimum share capital of ₹500.

What is the minimum deposit required to open an account?

+

The minimum deposit to open a savings account is ₹500. For Fixed Deposits, the minimum is ₹5,000 with tenures from 6 months to 5 years at competitive interest rates.

How quickly can I get a loan after becoming a member?

+

After your membership is confirmed, you can apply for a loan immediately. Personal and emergency loans are processed within 24–48 hours. Home and business loans take 5–7 working days.

What are the current Fixed Deposit interest rates?

+

Our FD rates range from 7.5% to 9.5% per annum depending on the tenure. Senior citizens get an additional 0.5% benefit. Please visit our office or contact us for the latest rates.

Is my money safe with Vara Siddhi?

+

Yes. Vara Siddhi is registered and regulated under the Karnataka Co-Operative Societies Act. All deposits are managed transparently and audited annually by certified auditors.
